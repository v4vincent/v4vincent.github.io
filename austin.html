Welcome to Austin, Texas

Dear residents and visitors of Austin,

I am honored to serve as the mayor of this vibrant and dynamic city. Whether you are a long-time resident or a first-time visitor, I want to extend a warm welcome to you.

Austin is a city that is known for its vibrant music scene, thriving tech industry, and unparalleled natural beauty. But what truly makes Austin special is the people who call it home. We are a diverse and inclusive community that values innovation, creativity, and a strong sense of community.

As your mayor, I am committed to ensuring that Austin remains a great place to live, work, and visit for generations to come. Together, we can continue to build a city that is both prosperous and equitable, where everyone has the opportunity to thrive.

Thank you for choosing Austin as your home or your destination. I look forward to working with you to make this city an even better place to be.

Sincerely,

Mayor Kirk Watson
