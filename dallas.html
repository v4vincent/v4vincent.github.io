Welcome to Dallas, Texas

Dear fellow Dallas residents and visitors,

On behalf of the city of Dallas, it is my pleasure to extend a warm welcome to you. Whether you're a newcomer to our city or a long-time resident, I hope you find Dallas to be a welcoming and vibrant place to call home.

Dallas is a city with a rich history and a bright future. We are a diverse and dynamic community, and we pride ourselves on being a hub for business, culture, and innovation. From our world-class museums and art galleries to our top-notch restaurants and nightlife, there's always something to discover and explore in Dallas.

As your mayor, I am committed to working tirelessly to ensure that Dallas continues to be a great place to live, work, and visit. I believe that by working together, we can build a city that is inclusive, prosperous, and resilient.

Thank you for choosing Dallas as your home or your destination. I look forward to working with you to make our city an even better place to be.

Sincerely,

Mayor Eric Johnson
